13.1 Analyze Frequency and Phase Error

Assume $Δ f$ is small Case 1: Frequency error, Δ f≠ 0, arbitrarily set δ θ

Case2: no Freq error, Δ f = 0, Δθ arbitrary.

Assume r(t)=SDSB-SC No noise in channel

$y(t) = m(t) cos(2π fct) cos(2π fcΔ f)t + Δθ)$ $= \frac{1}{2}m(t)[cos(2π Δ ft+Δ Θ)+cos(2π (2fc+Δ f)t+Δ θ)]$ $=\frac{1}{2}m(t)cos(2ΠΔ ft+Δ Θ) + \frac{1}{2} cos(2π (2fc + Δ f)t + Δ θ)$

$z(t) = \frac{1}{2} m(t) cos(2π Δ ft+ Δθ )$ case 1: $z(t) = \frac{1}{2} m(t) cos(2π Δ ft)$
Unacceptable, even if Δ f is very small

case 2: $z(t) = \frac{1}{2} m(t) cos(Δ θ)$ Acceptable if Δθ is small (much smaller then 90°)

13.1.1 Delta Θ does not affect the noise level.
